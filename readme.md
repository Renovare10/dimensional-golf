# Dimensional Golf
A silly 2D golfing game in Godot

## About
Golf through various holes while changing dimensions to stay on the course!
Your starting dimension might be 1 (color 1) and you use keys to change dimensions
maybe to 2 or 3. However, the course may not have ground in the different dimensions
and there by making timed moves important to avoid obstactles, stay on the ground,
or get to the actual hole. hopefully the dimensions stay translucent so you can kind
of see them while changing. Each of the dimensions should have their own unique color.

## Other ideas
 * Music levels where the music changes the dimensions or other timed themes in this
 * Portal holes that transport you to dimensions
 * Portal guns (act just like portal 2)
 * Slops by shading striped lines in the direction of the slope
 * Black holes that suck you in and destroy your ball
 * Moving platforms
